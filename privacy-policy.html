SemperClock Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

SemperClock is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Data That Stays on Your Devices

All workout data, exercise configurations, and results are stored locally on your device. SemperClock does not upload your personal data to any cloud service or third-party server. Your data is yours.

Heart Rate Data

SemperClock reads heart rate data from Apple Watch via HealthKit and from external Bluetooth heart rate monitors (such as the Polar H10). This data is used solely to display real-time heart rate during workouts and to calculate heart rate zone summaries in your results. Heart rate data is not stored beyond the workout session results on your device.

Bluetooth Connections

The app uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to connect to:

  • External heart rate monitors
  • Concept2 PM5 rowing machine monitors

These connections are used exclusively for real-time workout metrics. No data from these devices is transmitted off your local network.

Local Network Communication

When you use multi-device features (iPhone, Apple Watch, Apple TV), SemperClock communicates over your local Wi-Fi network using Apple's MultipeerConnectivity and WatchConnectivity frameworks. This data (workout state, heart rate, rowing metrics) travels only between your devices on your local network.

Remote Multiplayer

If you choose to use the remote workout feature, SemperClock connects to a relay server to coordinate workouts with participants on other networks. The only data transmitted is:

  • A display name you choose
  • Device type (iPhone, iPad, Apple TV)
  • Real-time workout state (heart rate, rowing metrics, workout progress)

This data is transient and is not stored on the relay server after the session ends. No account creation is required.

Microphone

SemperClock may request microphone access for voice command features during workouts. Audio is processed entirely on-device using Apple's Speech framework. No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

No Tracking or Analytics

SemperClock does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app does not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral data beyond what Apple may collect through standard App Store mechanisms.

No Account Required

SemperClock does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in with any service.

Children's Privacy

SemperClock does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the SemperClock GitHub repository.
