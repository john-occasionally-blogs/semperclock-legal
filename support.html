Common Topics

  • Bluetooth connections — Make sure your heart rate monitor or Concept2 PM5 is in pairing mode and within range. SemperClock uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).
  • Apple Watch sync — Both your iPhone and Apple Watch must be on the same Wi-Fi network. Ensure the SemperClock watch app is installed.
  • Apple TV display — Your iPhone and Apple TV must be on the same local network. The TV app will appear in the Bullpen when discovered.
  • Heart rate not showing — Check that you've granted HealthKit permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > SemperClock.
